Hi!

I'm Lara, a qualitative and design researcher based in Copenhagen.

I work at the intersection of people, technologies, and futures to explore how we might create more inclusive, caring, and critical ways of designing and understanding worlds. With a background in anthropology, Science and Technology studies and Human-Computer Interaction, I'm interested in understanding people's lived experiences and their contexts to challenge the status quo. My work often unfolds in complex or sensitive settings, from healthcare systems to public digital services, where I seek to understand tensions, needs, and potentials that shape people's everyday experiences.

I believe technology should support, not exploit. I'm drawn to spaces where complexity is welcomed, where care is part of the method, and where innovation means reimagining what's worth building.

I work with:

  • *Qualitative methods & analysis
  • *Participatory design & co-creation workshops
  • *Ethnography & fieldwork
  • *User research
  • *Speculative design & design fiction
  • *FemTech & care technologies
  • *Digital public services
  • *Critical approaches to innovation
  • *Storytelling & research communication
  • *Futures thinking & inclusive design
